Facilitators / Chioma Nwankwo

Chioma Nwankwo

Senior Facilitator - Sound Design

Audio Production & Sound Design

Biography

Award-winning sound designer and audio engineer known for creating immersive soundscapes. Chioma's work spans feature films, documentaries, and branded content, bringing stories to life through sound.

With 11+ years of experience in the film industry, Chioma has worked on numerous productions and collaborated with industry leaders. Their expertise in audio production & sound design has made them a sought-after professional in the field.

At Winlos Film Academy, Chioma brings a wealth of practical knowledge and a passion for mentoring the next generation of filmmakers. Their teaching style combines hands-on experience with industry insights, ensuring students receive training that is both comprehensive and relevant to current market demands.
