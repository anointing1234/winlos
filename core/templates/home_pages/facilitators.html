FACILITATORS

Meet Our Dedicated Team

Our facilitators are seasoned film professionals passionate about equipping the next generation of creatives. Each brings real world industry experience, artistic excellence, and a heart to mentor students in their filmmaking journey.

Industry Experts Who Teach

At Winlos Film Academy, you learn from active industry professionals who bring fresh insights, proven techniques, and real world experience into every class. Our facilitators are not just teachers—they're filmmakers, storytellers, and mentors committed to your growth.

Every course is designed and taught by specialists who have worked on major productions and continue to create compelling content. They understand the challenges of the industry and know what it takes to succeed.

Founder & Lead Facilitator

Rev. Ohis Ojeikere

Rev. Ohis Ojeikere is more than the founder of Winlos Academy. He is a creative director driven by a deep conviction of faith, story, and cultural transformation. For over a decade, he has served as a dynamic lead pastor, film director, and writer.

His philosophy is simple yet profound: "We are not just called to be consumers of culture, but creators of it."
