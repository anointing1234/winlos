About The Winlos

A Premier FilmSchool
for the Next Generation

  • Started back in 2015
  • 8 in-demand educational programs
  • Several notable Alumni’s

The Winlos Academy is Nigeria’s premier Christian film school, established to empower a new generation of filmmakers. We believe that the most powerful stories are those told with both technical mastery and spiritual integrity.

We are not just training filmmakers; we are commissioning cultural influencers. Our mission is to send forth creatives who will shape the narrative of Hollywood and the world, producing content that illuminates, inspires, and transforms culture for Christ.

At TWA, we don’t just teach you how to tell a story. We help you discover the story you were born to tell.

Faith Based

Training students to create content that reflects truth, hope, and moral clarity.

Excellence

We believe excellence is not just a professional standard, but a spiritual discipline.

Global Impact

Empowering Nigerian filmmakers to reach and influence international audiences.

Growth

Nurturing not just technical skills, but character, leadership and spiritual growth.
