ABOUT THE ACADEMY

A Premier Film School for the Next Generation

The Winlos Academy is Nigeria's premier Christian film school, established to empower a new generation of filmmakers. We believe that the most powerful stories are those told with both technical mastery and spiritual integrity.

We are not just training filmmakers; we are commissioning cultural influencers. Our mission is to send forth creatives who will shape the narrative of the world, producing content that illuminates, inspires, and transforms culture.

Excellence

Professional standards meet spiritual discipline.

Community

A supportive network of creative minds.

Faith-Based

Creating content that reflects truth and hope.

Global Impact

Reaching and influencing audiences worldwide.
