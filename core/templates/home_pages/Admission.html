Admissions

Enjoy a Simple, Stress-Free Admission Process and Unlock Endless Opportunities for Learning, Creativity, and Personal Growth. Join a vibrant network of passionate learners, supportive mentors, and like-minded creators.

Become a Winlos Academy Student

At The Winlos Academy, we believe that every creative gift is a calling—and every calling deserves the right environment to grow, flourish, and impact the world. Our admissions process is designed to be simple, welcoming, and accessible, ensuring that nothing stands between you and the future God has prepared for you.

Journey At

The Winlos Academy

01

The Call to Create

Deciding if the Winlos Academy is the right place for you. Explore our programs, missions with our community, and pray about your next step.

02

Answering the Call

Submit a compelling application that reflects your potential. Tell us your story, share your creative vision, and demonstrate your commitment.

03

The Shepherd's Review

Application review by Admissions Office. Our team carefully reviews each application and provides feedback within 2-3 weeks.

04

Crafting The Calling

Immersing in learning and transformative discovery into the community. Complete enrollment, cover your cohort, and prepare for transformation.

05

The Commissioning

Confidently enter the film industry and creative marketplace. Graduate equipped, empowered, and ready to impact the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our admissions process

We welcome students aged 18 and above. While there is no upper age limit, applicants under 18 must have parental consent and demonstrate the maturity required for our intensive programs.

No prior experience is required! We welcome beginners with a passion for storytelling. Our programs are designed to take you from fundamentals to professional-level skills, regardless of your starting point.

Each course is priced at $200 (or ₦200,000). You can enroll in individual courses or bundle multiple courses for a comprehensive education. Payment plans are available to make your investment more manageable.

Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid to qualified students. Scholarship applications are reviewed alongside admissions applications. Contact our admissions office for more details.

Late applications may be considered on a case by case basis, depending on space availability. We recommend contacting our admissions team immediately if you need an extension or missed the deadline.

We offer both full-time intensive programs and flexible part-time options. You can choose courses that fit your schedule and complete them at your own pace while maintaining the quality of education.
