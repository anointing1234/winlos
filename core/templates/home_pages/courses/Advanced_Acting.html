Why this program?

Acting for film is different from stage acting. The camera captures everything—every subtle gesture, every fleeting emotion. This course teaches you how to deliver authentic, powerful performances that translate beautifully on screen. You'll learn techniques used by professional actors, from character preparation to emotional access.

What you will learn

Screen acting vs. stage acting techniques
Emotional preparation and access
Character development and backstory
Working with scripts and sides
On-camera presence and confidence
Audition techniques for film

What can I do with it later?

Graduates can pursue careers as film actors, voice actors, commercial talent, or acting coaches. You'll be prepared for auditions and professional film sets.

Curriculum

Module 1: Foundations

Camera vs. stage acting

Finding truth in performance

Emotional availability

Module 2: Character Work

Building a character from script

Physical and vocal choices

Staying in the moment

Module 3: Professional Practice

Audition preparation

Working with directors

On-set professionalism
