ABOUT WINLOS FILM ACADEMY

Faith and creativity in
motion

We've created a faith-based film academy committed to equipping creatives, empowering storytellers, and shining God's light through film.

Our community transforms lives through powerful visual storytelling, bringing together emerging filmmakers with industry professionals to create authentic, impactful content that glorifies God.

“We are not just called to be consumers of culture, but creators of it.”

— Rev. Ohis Ojeikere, Founder

Experience is at the heart of everything we do

Founded with a vision to transform the film industry through faith, Winlos Film Academy is renowned for our experiential learning model, high-impact training, deep industry partnerships, and kingdom-focused approach.

From day one, we've pursued innovative ways of teaching filmmaking that place a premium on hands-on experience and authentic engagement with the craft. Today, our signature approach empowers not only students, but facilitators, alumni, and partners to create transformative stories that pursue impact.

Our Story

Winlos Film Academy was born from a simple but profound conviction: the church needs better storytellers. Rev. Ohis Ojeikere, a lead pastor, film director, and writer, recognized a gap between the quality of secular film content and faith-based productions.

Rather than critiquing from the sidelines, he decided to do something about it. He founded Winlos Film Academy to train a new generation of creatives who could compete at the highest levels of the industry while remaining anchored in biblical truth.

Today, Winlos is more than a film school. It's a movement a community of artists, directors, writers, cinematographers, and producers who believe that stories have the power to change hearts, shift culture, and glorify God.

We don't just teach technical skills. We cultivate character, vision, and excellence. We believe that the best films come from the best storytellers people who know their craft, understand their calling, and are committed to using their gifts for God's glory.

Vision

To raise a generation of skilled filmmakers who use storytelling and visual media to glorify God, transform lives, and advance the Kingdom through film.

Mission

To equip purpose-driven creatives with practical filmmaking skills, strong storytelling foundations, and spiritual values, empowering them to create films that communicate truth, inspire faith, and make lasting impact across cultures and generations.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do from how we teach to how we create

Faith Centered

Every lesson, project, and conversation is rooted in biblical truth and Kingdom values.

Excellence

Community

Filmmaking is collaborative. We foster a culture of mutual support, mentorship, and creative partnership.

Impact

We create stories that move audiences, challenge perspectives, and ultimately point people to Jesus.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a world where faith-based films compete at the highest level winning awards, dominating box offices, and shifting cultural narratives. We see graduates of Winlos Film Academy leading production companies, directing blockbuster films, and creating content that impacts millions.

But more than professional success, we dream of a movement where Christian creatives refuse to settle for mediocrity. Where excellence and faith go hand in hand. Where the stories we tell don't just entertain they transform.

This is the legacy we're building at Winlos Film Academy. And we invite you to be part of it.
